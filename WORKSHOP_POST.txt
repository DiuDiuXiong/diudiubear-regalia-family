TITLE
Diu Diu Bear's Regalia Arsenal

SHORT DESCRIPTION
Adds two modular 7.62x51 Regalia rifles, exclusive attachments, custom sounds, and Diu Diu Bear as a bunker trader beside the Networker.

FULL WORKSHOP DESCRIPTION
Meet Diu Diu Bear — the bunker’s new specialist in colourful, high-powered hardware.

This mod adds the Regalia weapon family: two modular 7.62x51 battle rifles with their own exclusive attachment ecosystem and custom audio.

FEATURES
• Regalia — a configurable automatic battle rifle with a grounded base setup.
• Regalia Prism — a faster, stronger and more controllable colourful variant.
• Compact, Standard and Grand stocks.
• Standard and Grand grips, including colourful Prism variants.
• Compact brake, Standard compensator and Grand suppressor.
• A dedicated 30-round Regalia magazine.
• Custom firing, suppressed and reload sounds.
• Diu Diu Bear, a custom polar-bear trader beside the Networker in the bunker.

WHERE TO FIND THE ITEMS
Talk to Diu Diu Bear beside the Networker. The shop stocks both receivers, all available attachments and 7.62x51 ammunition.

COMPATIBILITY
The mod uses its own wz_ddb_ internal namespace to reduce ID conflicts. Avoid enabling a local development copy and the subscribed Workshop copy simultaneously.
