FIRST UPLOAD CHECKLIST
1. Keep the folder name: diudiubear_regalia_family
2. Confirm Steam is running and logged into the intended account.
3. Open ZERO Sievert's Mod Manager and choose Upload for this folder.
4. The first upload must use meta.ini without workshop_id.
5. After Steam creates the Workshop item, add this line below [leave blank]:
   workshop_id=YOUR_NEW_WORKSHOP_ID
6. Keep that new ID for all future updates.
7. Test the subscribed Workshop copy with the local copy disabled.
